What is Fintech?

Fintech, or financial technology is the use of computer programs and other technology to support or enable banking and financial services.

A Brief History:
The fintech industry emerged in the 20th century and at its beginning was used mainly large investment and baking companies. Now, however fintech companies are creating more consumer based services. such as money transfers, currency exchange, and small business banking.
